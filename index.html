The Five Emotional Stages to Web Deverlopment

1. The I can't fix this stage

This stage normally kicks in when you are presented with some messy, broken or just downright unintelligible project, take a huge breath, grab some coffee and look at the below meme, then get back to it!

2. The Crises of Confidence

This stage normally happens when you have just started to tweak your code and everything goes wonky. Check out the below links, there are tons of online resources that can help you and these are just a few to get you started.

  • w3schools
  • Stack overflow
  • MDN web docs

3. The Questioning of your Career Choices stage

This happens when you feel you are well and truely stuck, you feel like you are going around in circles and nothing is working. Take another deep breath, re-read the above and follow the instructions.

4. The Extenstional Crises

To be fair this happens to all of us at one time or another, check this link out if anything it will make you laugh about web devlopement, once you feel better about exsiting, get back to it!

5. The 'Oh! It was a typo' stage

This is when you have been to hell and back with your code, everything you have done, everyone you have asked (including your rubber duck) haven't been able to help and in sweaty desperation you have chugged some luke warm coffee and re-read your code in every which direction and found...... GASP! A typo, you fix it and everything now works!
